About

I’m an AI/ML engineer with a Dr.-Ing. and around eight years building ML systems that have to hold up in production. I studied mechanical engineering at TU Darmstadt and earned my doctorate at the University of Stuttgart in collaboration with Bosch. Today I build agentic AI for capital markets at Deutsche Börse; before that, seven years at Bosch, from autonomous driving simulation and perception to LLM and agentic systems. This site is where I keep what I’m working on. For CV-level detail, ask the agent above.
