about

Research-grade methods, production systems.

Anthony is an AI/ML engineer with a doctorate in engineering and roughly eight years building perception and simulation systems at scale. His background sits where classical computer vision, sensor modeling, and modern generative AI meet.

Today he works in the CTO area at Deutsche Börse, designing agentic AI systems for capital-markets infrastructure. Before that, years at Robert Bosch on autonomous driving simulation: synthetic data, Radar/Lidar sensor models, perception, and neural rendering. He likes problems where research methods have to survive contact with real systems.
